## How to Use

1. **Open** `index.html` in Safari on your iPad or iPhone
2. **Click "Start camera"** to see a preview of your camera
3. **Click "Go Full Screen"** to enter recording mode
4. **Edit your script** in the Script text area
5. **Click "Start recording"** to begin recording with auto-scroll
6. **Click the red "Stop Recording" button** when finished
7. **Download** your video clip

## Controls

| Button | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Start camera | Opens camera preview |
| Flip camera | Switch front/back camera |
| Start recording | Begins video recording |
| Stop recording | Stops and saves recording |
| Play scroll | Starts auto-scrolling script |
| Reset | Resets scroll position to top |
| Mirror text | Flips text horizontally |
| Speed slider | Adjusts scroll speed |

## Requirements

- Modern web browser (Safari recommended for iOS)

## Tips

- Run fullscreen for best experience
- Keep your gaze near the camera lens for natural eye contact
- Adjust scroll speed to match your reading pace
- Use Mirror mode with a teleprompter beam splitter
